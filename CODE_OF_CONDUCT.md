# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1. The full text is available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported
to the project maintainer, **[@jackhp95](https://github.com/jackhp95)**, privately via
GitHub's [private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/jackhp95/elm-m3e/security/advisories/new)
or the contact on the maintainer's GitHub profile. All complaints will be reviewed
and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to respect the
privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

This document is a pointer to Contributor Covenant 2.1; refer to the linked text for
the complete community standards, enforcement guidelines, and attribution.
